About me

I’m a fifth-year student at University of Jyväskylä majoring in mathematical information technology. I’m driven about doing robust and maintainable code - building software that matters. That has lead me to explore topics like functional programming and best practices for software development.

Regarding my musical endeavors, I’m part of the band This Horse. I dabble with making music solo (too), but I haven’t dared to put out anything yet.

In my free time I do all sorts of nerdy stuff like playing Magic the Gathering (kitchen table tier), tabletop RPGs and, of course, video games.

I hope you enjoy your stay in my digital garden!
