About me

I'm a third-year student at University of Jyväskylä majoring in information technology, minor in mathematics. I'm interested in programming, audio and music; ideally combining all three. I've discovered Linux to be a cool thing and I like to learn about it's inner workings. As low-level languages like C and C++ are used a lot in audio programming, it's probably no surprise that systems level programming intrigues me as well.

Regarding my musical endeavors: I'm part of the band This Horse. I dabble with making music solo (too), but I haven't dared to put out anything yet.

On my free time I do all sorts of nerdy stuff like playing Magic the Gathering (kitchen table tier), tabletop RPGs and, of course, videogames.
