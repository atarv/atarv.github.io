About me

I’m a sixth-year student at University of Jyväskylä majoring in mathematical information technology. I’m driven about doing robust and maintainable code — building software that matters. That has lead me to explore topics like functional programming, programming languages and best practices of software development.

In my free time I do all sorts of nerdy stuff like playing Magic the Gathering (kitchen table tier), tabletop RPGs and, of course, video games.

I also play the guitar and drums (on the rare chance I get to be around a drum set). I dabble with making music, but I haven’t dared to put out anything yet. Maybe you’ll see a Soundcloud link here someday.
